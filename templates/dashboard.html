Sector Score Interpretation

Sector Score = 0.7 × Breadth + 0.3 × Momentum

Breadth = % of stocks advancing in the sector today.
Momentum = consistency of recent positive participation.

Higher scores indicate stronger institutional participation and sector leadership.

Score Range Meaning Trading Interpretation
70+ Very Strong Sector Institutional accumulation / leadership sector
60–70 Strong Sector Trending sector with broad participation
50–60 Neutral / Rotating Market indecision, watch for breakout
40–50 Weak Sector Limited participation, avoid new longs
<40 Very Weak Sector Distribution or sector underperforming
Emerging Sector Opportunities

Emerging sectors are identified using the following conditions:

  • Sector Score between 50 and 70 Sector showing improving strength but not yet a market leader.
  • Positive Momentum Recent participation improving within the sector.
  • Healthy Breadth At least ~40% of stocks advancing in the sector.

These sectors often represent early stage sector rotation where institutional money begins shifting before a sector becomes a clear market leader.

Stage Interpretation Typical Action
Emerging Strength improving Early opportunity
Leading Strong participation Trend continuation
Weakening Momentum slowing Watch for rotation
Lagging Weak participation Avoid longs
Fastest Strengthening Sectors

This section identifies sectors where momentum and participation are increasing rapidly. These sectors often represent early institutional accumulation before they become clear market leaders.

Sectors qualify as Fastest Strengthening when:

  • Momentum ≥ 10
    Sector participation improving rapidly.
  • Sector Score ≥ 50
    Sector already showing reasonable strength.
  • High momentum relative to other sectors
    Top sectors sorted by momentum acceleration.
Signal Interpretation Typical Market Behaviour
Fastest Strengthening Momentum expanding rapidly Sector may soon become a leader
Emerging Strength improving gradually Early stage sector rotation
Leading Strong participation Established sector trend
Weakening Momentum fading Possible profit booking

These signals help identify where institutional capital may be rotating next.
